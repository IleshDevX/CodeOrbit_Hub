What is Detectron2?

Detectron2 is Facebook AI Research's next-generation library that provides state-of-the-art detection and segmentation algorithms. It is a ground-up rewrite of the original Detectron, designed to be more flexible, extensible, and faster.

Enhancing surveillance and threat detection systems.

Creating immersive and interactive virtual experiences.

Core Capabilities

Detectron2 is packed with powerful features to tackle a wide range of computer vision tasks.

Object Detection

Identify and locate objects within an image with bounding boxes.

Instance Segmentation

Detect objects and precisely segment their boundaries at the pixel level.

Keypoint Detection

Detect specific points of interest on objects, like human joints.

Panoptic Segmentation

Combines instance and semantic segmentation for a complete scene understanding.

DensePose

Map all human pixels of an RGB image to the 3D surface of the human body.

Custom Training Support

Easily train models on your own custom datasets with minimal effort.
