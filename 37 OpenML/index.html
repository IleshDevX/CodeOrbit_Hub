About OpenML

The open platform for machine learning collaboration

OpenML is an open science platform for machine learning that allows researchers to share datasets, algorithms, and experiments. It provides a collaborative environment where you can discover, download, and analyze thousands of datasets and benchmark your models.

Join a global community of researchers and data scientists working together to advance machine learning research.
