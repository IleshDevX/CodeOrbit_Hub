NLTK: The Natural Language Toolkit for Learning

A leading open-source platform for building Python programs to work with human language data, ideal for education, research, and prototyping.

Learn More Try Demos

Discover the Power of NLTK

NLTK is a powerful open-source library created for teaching and research in Natural Language Processing. It provides easy-to-use interfaces to over 50 corpora and lexical resources, along with a suite of text processing libraries for classification, tokenization, stemming, tagging, parsing, and semantic reasoning. It's the perfect tool for students, educators, and researchers to dive into the world of NLP.

Core Capabilities

Tokenization & Stemming

Break down text into words or sentences (tokens) and reduce words to their root forms (stems) with robust algorithms.

Part-of-Speech Tagging

Automatically identify and label words as nouns, verbs, adjectives, etc., forming the basis for further analysis.

Named Entity Recognition (NER)

Locate and classify named entities in text into pre-defined categories such as persons, organizations, and locations.

Parsing & Syntax Trees

Analyze grammatical structure and generate syntax trees to understand sentence composition and relationships.

Corpora & Lexical Resources

Access a vast collection of text corpora and lexical databases like WordNet for comprehensive language studies.

Text Classification

Train machine learning models to classify text into categories, essential for sentiment analysis and topic modeling.
