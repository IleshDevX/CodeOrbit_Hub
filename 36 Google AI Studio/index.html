What is AI Studio?

Google AI Studio is an intuitive, web-based tool designed for developers, researchers, and AI enthusiasts. It provides a seamless interface to prototype and experiment with Google's latest generative AI models, including the powerful Gemini family, without needing to write extensive code.

It serves as a creative and technical sandbox where you can test prompts, fine-tune model behavior, and rapidly develop AI-powered applications. Whether you're learning the fundamentals of AI or building a complex prototype, AI Studio offers the resources you need, completely free of charge.
