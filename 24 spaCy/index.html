What is spaCy?

spaCy is an open-source software library for advanced Natural Language Processing, written in Python and Cython. It's designed to be fast, streamlined, and ready for real-world applications. From research to production, spaCy helps you build systems that understand language.

Powerful Features Out-of-the-Box

spaCy is packed with features to handle any NLP challenge.

Tokenization

Segmenting text into words, punctuation, and other meaningful units. spaCy's tokenizer is fast, accurate, and language-specific.

Named Entity Recognition (NER)

Identify and classify real-world objects like people, organizations, locations, and dates in your text.

Part-of-Speech Tagging

Assign grammatical labels like noun, verb, and adjective to each token, providing deep linguistic insight.

Dependency Parsing

Analyze the grammatical structure of a sentence to understand relationships between words.

Pre-trained Pipelines

Get started instantly with statistical models for over 20 languages, supporting a variety of NLP tasks.

Speed & Efficiency

Written from the ground up in Cython, spaCy is one of the fastest NLP libraries in the world.
