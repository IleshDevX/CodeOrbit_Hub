What is Vosk?

Vosk is an open-source, offline speech recognition toolkit. It enables speech recognition for a wide variety of platforms and devices, from servers to small edge devices like Raspberry Pi, without needing an internet connection.

Key Features

Offline Operation

Works entirely on-device, ensuring privacy and low latency.

Lightweight Models

Small models (~50Mb) that run efficiently on resource-constrained devices.

High Accuracy

Provides excellent recognition accuracy with speaker adaptation features.

Multilingual

Supports over 20 languages and dialects with a simple model change.
